About Us

Established in 2035 B.S., Tapasi Baba Secondary School & College is a respected community-based institution in Dhanusha, Nepal. Affiliated with NEB and CTEVT, the school provides education from ECD to Grade 12 along with Pre-Diploma in Electrical Engineering. We focus on academic excellence, discipline, moral values, leadership development, and technical skills. Our mission is to prepare responsible citizens equipped with knowledge and character.

Principal's Message

Welcome to Tapasi Baba Secondary School & College. Our goal is to provide quality education in a supportive and disciplined environment. We believe education is the foundation of national development. Together, we strive for excellence and innovation. - Madan Kumar Mahara (Head Teacher)
